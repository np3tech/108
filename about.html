Who Am I?

I'm Dipak Lungeli, a multi-talented individual from Kawasoti, Nepal. I'm a passionate web developer, lyrical rapper, music producer, and writer. My journey blends creativity, culture, and code, aiming to express emotion and truth through technology and words.

My Interests & Passions

I am deeply interested in historical, philosophical, and spiritual subjects. Whether it's understanding ancient cultures or exploring futuristic AI systems, I love discovering knowledge that connects people across time and space. I believe in continuous learning and exploring the depths of human thought and technological advancement.

Skills & Expertise

As a developer, I primarily work with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, and Lua. In music production, I leverage tools like FL Studio and REAPER. Additionally, I write engaging poems, ghazals, stories, and thought-provoking articles, sharing my content with my audience on platforms like YouTube and Facebook.

My Vision

I believe in using digital tools to empower expression and enhance accessibility. One of my core goals is to build software and experiences that are inclusive for visually impaired users, while also offering emotional and creative value through my writing and music. I envision a digital space where technology serves humanity's creative and communicative needs.

Life Philosophy

For me, curiosity is life's fuel. I live by the idea that "Expectation kills happiness," and instead, I focus on growth, exploration, and contributing something meaningful to the world around me. This philosophy guides my personal and professional pursuits, driving me to constantly learn, create, and inspire.
